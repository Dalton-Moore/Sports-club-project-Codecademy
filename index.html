About Riding Safety

Welcome To The SLC Bike Night Club

Meet up. Ride Bikes. Have Fun.

Come ride bikes with a community whose focus is having fun and enjoying a ride around town.

SLC Bike Night Club is free for everyone, we meet up at 2nd and 2nd every Sunday night at 7pm.

For safety we always recommend wearing a helmet, and a front and rear LED light.

We can't wait to see you out there!
